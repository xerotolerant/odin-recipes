Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe will make you the talk of the town.

Follow these steps to make a your lasagna delicious, tender and firm. Don't worry about the calories, we all have to die from something.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dry lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 lb Mosarella (sheredded)
  • 1/2 lb Aged Cheddar (shredded)
  • 1 lb groud beef
  • 500g Tomato sauce
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 5 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp flour

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in well salted water until al dente. Drain pasta conserving a quarter cup of the pasta water
  2. Place butter into pan over medium high heat. Once butter melts and begins to froth, producing a nutty aroma add flour and stir until combined
  3. Add milk and whisk until smooth
  4. Gradually add cheeses and whisk until combined. If it becomes stuff stir in some of the pasta water
  5. Brown Beef in pan. once beef is browned add Tomato sauce and stir. Add desired spices and allow to simmer for 30 minutes. Allow to reduce to spreadable consistency
  6. Place ingredients into tray for baking. Use meat, noodles, cheese in layers in that order until you are at the top with meat.
  7. Sprinkle a healthy amount of shredded cheese over the top
  8. Make in over at 350 for 45 minutes
  9. Allow to cool and set for 15 minutes before serving
